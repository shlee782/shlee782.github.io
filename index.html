Hello!

I am a Ph.D. student at Yonsei University (advisor: Prof. Jiwon Seo). I have a comprehensive understanding of signal processing, deep learning, and speech recognition, developed through my Ph.D. studies on radar-based silent speech recognition. I am eager to apply my expertise on tackling speech-related problems. Currently, I am interested in speaker recognition.
